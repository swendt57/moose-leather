Moose Leather

My name is Steve Wendt and I have been working with leather since 1989. In the 90's I owned Moose Leather which was a store on Upas St here in San Diego. Besides custom work, I also had off-the-rack items, and offered alterations and repair. After a 20-year stint at Qualcomm, I am resurrecting my leather business.

I work out of my home and can alter, repair, or create something new for you. I have extensive experience with chaps, vests, pants, shirts, harnesses, and the like. You can see some of my original work throughout this site. Occasionally I will have items for sale in Off-The-Rack. And, who knows, maybe I'll figure out a way to bring back our consignment rack that was quite popular at the time.

Feel free to text or call me on my cell at (619) 964-1865 or email me at steve@moose-leather.com and let me create your vision!
